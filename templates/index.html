How does it work?

GenerosiMe contains a database of local charitable organizations, such as Goodwill, The Salvation Army, homeless shelters, etc. When you register and enter your location, we compile a list of nearby charities that you can donate to, and allow you to choose which one you would like to support. Then, once your order is placed, a nearby driver provided by our partners at Postmates will stop by to pick up your donation and deliver it to the designated location. Enjoy the easiest and most worry-free donation of your life!

How do I get started?

Click our "Get Started" button above, and fill out a simple registration form containing basic contact information. Then, to make an order, click our "Pickup" button (also above). This will take you to a form where you fill out which donation center you would like to support and a few details about your donation. Then simply click "Quote" for a delivery fee estimate and then "Submit" to send your order in. It's that simple! Each order can be completed in about a minute or less.
